Poset

Poset is a GAP package specialized for working with finite geometrical partially ordered sets (posets) while accounting for their inherent symmetries.

Overview

  • Symmetry Handling: Designed to process posets with complex symmetry groups.
  • Geometric Focus: Specifically tailored for posets arising from geometric structures like polytopes and configurations.
  • Environment: Runs within the GAP (Groups, Algorithms, Programming) computational discrete algebra system.
