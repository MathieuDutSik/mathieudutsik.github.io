FEM to FD

  • Sometimes we have a finite difference grid and we need to obtain from it a finite felement mesh for another purpose.
  • The program FEM_to_FD does that:
    • In the middle of the ocean it simply splits squares in two.
    • But it adds points near the coast so that no straits is closed and no isthmus opened.
