LP Bathymetry

  • A finite difference model (like ROMS) is called terrain following if the number of vertical levels is fixed and their relative position varies with the bathymetry
  • The stability of such models depends, among many other things, on the roughness of the bathymetry
  • The program availabe here gives a method for finding the bathymetry closest to the real bathymetry while satisfying to the roughness factor constraint.
  • The manual is available here.
  • The matlab scripts with some examples are available here.
  • The linear programs are solved using the program lp_solve.
