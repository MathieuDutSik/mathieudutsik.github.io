Some perfect cones

  • In a given dimension n, there are only a finite number of orbits of perfect cones of rank k.
  • In order to solve the problem considered in Smoothness and singularities of the perfect form compactification of Ag we needed to enumerate the perfect cones of rank up to 9.
  • The perfect cones of dimension n and rank n correspond to certain configurations of shortest vectors and are enumerated for dimension n<=9 in On classifying Minkowskian sublattices
    • and references therein.
  • The faces of the cones of dimension at most 7 are enumerated in Perfect forms and the cohomology of modular groups
  • The faces of rank 9 and dimension 8 are enumerated in Smoothness and singularities of the perfect form compactification of Ag.
  • Merged data set is available at DATApaper.tar.gz. Data can be read in GAP by doing ReadAsFunction(File)() with File one of the files of the archive.
