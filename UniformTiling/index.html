Uniform Partition of 3-space

A tiling is defined as a uniform partition of 3-space if it is composed of Archimedean polyhedra and its symmetry group is transitive on its vertices. This page provides visual representations, Delaney symbols, and technical data for these structures.

Historical Context & Corrections

Correct enumerations can be found in the works of Deza & Shtogrin (2000), Grünbaum (1996), and Johnson (1991). Historical listings by Andreini, Critchlow, and others often contained errors or omissions, which this resource aims to clarify.

Technical Details

  • Visualization: Pictures generated using 3dt by Olaf Delgado.
  • Encoding: Structures are encoded using Delaney symbols.
  • Space Groups: Referred to standard crystallographic nomenclature (see Wikipedia).
  • Scripts: Scripts for generating these tilings are available here.

The 28 Uniform Partitions

Structure 1 (DS-5, A-2, J-21)

Delaunay tessellation of the A3 lattice.

  • Composition: Tetrahedra & Octahedra (Ratio 1:2)
  • Space Group: Fm-3m
  • Delaney symbol

Structure 2 (DS-24, A-2', J-52)

One of only two vertex-transitive tessellations in the tetrahedron-octahedron continuum.

  • Composition: Tetrahedra & Octahedra (Ratio 1:2)
  • Space Group: P63/mmc
  • Delaney symbol

Structure 3 (DS-25, J-61)

Created by inserting a layer of Prism3 between layers of structure 1.

  • Composition: Tetrahedron, Octahedron, Prism3 (2:1:3)
  • Space Group: R-3m
  • Delaney symbol

Structure 4 (DS-26, J-62)

Created by inserting a layer of Prism3 between layers of structure 2.

  • Composition: Tetrahedron, Octahedron, Prism3 (2:1:3)
  • Space Group: P63/mmc
  • Delaney symbol

Structure 5 (DS-19, A-16, J-23)

  • Composition: Tetrahedron, Rhombicuboctahedron, Cube (2:1:1)
  • Space Group: Fm-3m
  • Delaney symbol

Structure 6 (DS-6, A-13, J-25)

  • Composition: Tetrahedron, Truncated Tetrahedron (1:1)
  • Space Group: Fd-3m
  • Delaney symbol

Structure 7 (DS-8, A-15, J-12)

Structure 11 (DS-3, A-2, J-21)

Structure 15 (DS-28, J-64)
