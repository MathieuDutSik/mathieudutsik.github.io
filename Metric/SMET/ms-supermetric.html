(m,s)-super-metrics cone

  • An (m,s)-super-metric d is a function defined over all (m+1)-uples of {1,...,n} such that
    • d(x_1,....,x_[m+1]) is invariant by permutation of the x_i
    • d(x_1,....,x_[m+1])=0 if two x_i are identical
    • d(x_1,....,x_[m+1])>=0
    • sd(x_1,....,x_[m+1])<=sum_{i=1}^{m+1} d(x_1,....,x_[i-1},x_[i+1],...,x_[m+1])
  • The (m,s)-super-metric cone SMET^[m,s]_n is the polyhedral cone of all (m,s)-super-metrics
  • The (m,s)-super-cut cone SCUT^[m,s]_n is the positive span of all (0,1)-valued extreme rays of SMET^[m,s]_n
