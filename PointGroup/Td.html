The group
This is the group of isometries preserving a tetrahedron

Features

  • Algebraic structure: it is the symmetric group on 4 elements (24 elements)
  • Kind of elements
    • 1 element: identity,
    • 3 elements: rotation of angle pi going throught pairs of opposite edges of the tetrahedron,
    • 8 elements: rotation of angle 2pi/3 or 4pi/3 with axis going throught vertices and opposite faces.
    • 6 elements: symmetries containing one edge and the middle of opposite edge
    • 6 elements: composition of rotation of angle pi/4 or -pi/4 and plane symmetry.
  • Examples
    • First Fullerene of symmetry Td
