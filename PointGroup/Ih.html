The group
This is the group of isometries preserving a regular Dodecahedron

Features

  • Algebraic structure: it is the direct product of Alt(5) and of Z2, in Coxeter nomenclature it is called H3 (120 elements)
  • Kind of elements
    • 1 element: identity,
    • 6x4 elements: rotation of order 5 on six possible axis
    • 10x2 elements: rotation of order 3 on ten possible axis
    • 15 elements: rotation of order 2 on 15 possible axis
    • 15 elements: plane symmetry
    • 45 elements: composition of rotation and plane symmetry
