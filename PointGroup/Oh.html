The group
This is the group of isometries preserving a regular Cube

Features

  • Algebraic structure: semidirect product of Sym(3) and of Z2xZ2xZ2 (48 elements)
  • Kind of elements
    • 1 element: identity,
    • 4x2 elements: rotations of order 3 around four possible axis
    • 3x3 elements: rotation of order 4 around three possible axis
    • 6 elements: rotation of order 2 around six possible axis
    • 9 elements: plane symmetry
    • 15 elements: composition of rotation and of plane symmetry
