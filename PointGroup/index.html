Point Groups

  • Point Groups are finite subgroup of the group O3 of isometries of the three dimensional space
  • They are classified in several infinite families and a few exceptional cases.
  • The algebraic symmetry group of 3-connected planar graphs is realized as the isometry group of a polytope and so as a point group.
  • Some classification results are obtained in Some Classification results
