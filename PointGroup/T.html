The series
This is the group of rotation preserving a tetrahedron

Features

  • Algebraic structure: it is the alternating group on 4 elements (12 elements)
  • Kind of elements
    • 1 element: identity,
    • 3 elements: rotation of angle pi going throught pairs of opposite edges of the tetrahedron,
    • 8 elements: rotation of angle 2pi/3 or 4pi/3 with axis going throught vertices and opposite faces.
  • Examples
    • First Fullerene of symmetry T
