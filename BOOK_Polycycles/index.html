Programs used for computations

All those depends on the plangraph package for planar graphs
  • EnumerationIsohedral.g Enumeration of isohedral and isogonal (r,q)-polycycles
  • Polycycle.tar.gz Enumeration of elementary ({2,3},5)-polycycles and ({3,4,5},3)-polycycles
  • StrictlyFaceRegular.tar.gz Enumeration of strictly face regular spheres
  • FrankKasper.tar.gz Enumeration of Frank Kasper ({5,q},3)-spheres
  • QR1.tar.gz Enumeration of ({5,b},3)-spheres bR1
  • 48sphere_8R4.tar.gz Partial enumeration of ({4,8}, 3)-spheres 8R4
