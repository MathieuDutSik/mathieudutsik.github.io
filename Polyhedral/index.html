Polyhedral

The Polyhedral package is a high-performance library devoted to the study of polytopes and lattices, with a strong focus on symmetry-assisted computations.

Main Functionalities

  • Symmetry-Reduced Dual Description: Efficiently computes dual descriptions of polytopes by leveraging symmetries.
  • Geometric Properties: Computes automorphism groups, volumes, and K-skeletons of polytopes.
  • Advanced Constructions: Full support for Wythoff constructions.
  • Lattice Theory: Computes Delaunay tessellations for lattices in Rn.
  • Space Analysis: Tools for dealing with L-type domains (spaces of lattices).
  • Unimodular Systems: Computation with unimodular vector systems.
  • Group Theory: Recognition of affine and spherical Coxeter Dynkin diagrams.
  • T-Spaces: Enumeration of perfect forms in T-spaces.
